Ingredients

  • Dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, nutmeg and cinnamon

  • Wet ingredients: milk, egg, vanilla extract, and mashed bananas

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron

  2. Sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, nutmeg and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl

  3. Whisk in milk, egg, vanilla and mashed bananas and mix until just incorporated (you don't want to over-mix

  4. Pour batter onto greased waffle iron and cook until golden. Serve warm
